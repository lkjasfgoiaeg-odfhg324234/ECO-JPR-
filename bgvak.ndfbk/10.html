10. Urban Planning and Smart Cities

Description:

Data analytics and simulation tools used to design urban spaces that optimize resource usage, minimize waste, and improve quality of life.

Examples:

Cities like Barcelona use smart sensors to monitor traffic and optimize waste collection, while Singapore employs data analytics for urban planning.

Impact:

Smart city initiatives reduce congestion, lower energy consumption, and improve public transportation, creating more sustainable urban environments.
