Digital Solutions on Sustainable Change

2. Digital Supply Chain Transparency

Description:

Blockchain technology can create immutable records of transactions and product journeys, ensuring that every step of the supply chain is documented.

Examples:

Companies like IBM and Walmart use blockchain to trace food products, ensuring safety and sustainability.

Impact:

This transparency helps consumers make informed choices, reduces unethical practices, and encourages companies to adopt sustainable sourcing methods, ultimately leading to lower environmental impacts.
