8. E-Learning Platforms on Sustainability

Description:

Online courses and resources designed to educate users about sustainability practices and environmental issues.

Examples:

Platforms like Coursera and edX offer courses from universities on sustainability, while organizations like the UN offer resources on sustainable development.

Impact:

Education empowers individuals and businesses to implement sustainable practices, leading to widespread behavioral change and increased community engagement in sustainability efforts.
