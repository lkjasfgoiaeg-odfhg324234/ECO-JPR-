Digital Solutions on Sustainable Change

1.Smart Energy Management Systems

Description:

These systems use Internet of Things (IoT) devices, such as smart thermostats and energy monitors, to track and manage energy consumption in real time.

Examples:

Products like Nest or Ecobee enable users to optimize heating and cooling based on their habits. Companies like EnergyHub provide platforms that integrate various smart devices.

Impact:

By adjusting energy usage based on demand, these systems can reduce energy costs and greenhouse gas emissions. Users can also access insights on their energy consumption patterns, encouraging more sustainable practices.
